Tell us why you are a Proud Member

Join these KU alumni and friends who are proud members of the KU Alumni Association. Association membership is the best way to strengthen KU and show your Jayhawk pride. Help us spread the word!

"I want to stay involved. I want to stay attached to the University in whatever way possible, so I support it as much as I can."—Rob Riggle, Proud Member, Actor and Comedian

"When the KU Alumni Association comes to the Bay Area for an event, it's very useful to meet other alumni and realize we have connections not only to the school but to all these people out in the field."—Brian McClendon, Proud Member, Vice President of Google Maps
