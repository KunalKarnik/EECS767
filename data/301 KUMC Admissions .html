KUMC Admissions

Each academic program at KUMC has unique admissions requirements and instructions. Please visit the program’s website and review the admission information.

International Applicant Information

The following programs utilize national application services. Once KUMC receives your application from the national service, you will be contacted about how to complete the KUMC online supplemental application:

  • Dietetic Internship Certificate Program
  • Doctor of Medicine (MD)
  • Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT)
  • Master of Occupational Therapy (MOT)

All other undergraduate, graduate, and certificate programs utilize KUMC’s online application for admission. Please visit the program’s website for application deadlines.

KUMC Online Application for Admission and General Instructions

Transcripts

For undergraduate and graduate programs, please send official transcripts to:

KUMC Office of the Registrar/Student Admissions
MS 4029
3901 Rainbow Blvd
Kansas City, KS 66160

or if your institution offers electronic delivery of official transcripts, they can be sent to kumcadmissions@kumc.edu.

In order for a transcript to be considered official, it must be sent directly from the previous institution to KUMC. Transcripts issued to the student are not considered official. During the application process, students can submit unofficial transcripts for application reviews.

Doctor of Medicine applicants send their transcripts directly to School of Medicine Admissions.

GRE/TOEFL Scores

KUMC’s GRE/TOEFL code is 6895 to ensure that KUMC receives your score.
