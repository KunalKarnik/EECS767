Resources

Home » Resources

 

Welcome

The KU network is 300,000 strong. Successful alumni include authors, a Nobel-prize winner, cancer-curing scientists, NASA astronauts, actors and more. The list goes on and on. Association membership helps you find friendly faces in your field with access to professional development resources.

My Profile

Update your alumni record online so you can always stay in touch with KU. Click here to log in, or create your own username and password here. Step-by-step instructions are available here.

Online Directory

Need to find a friend, an old roommate, a classmate or a professional in your field? The Online Directory allows members to search more than 200,000 records by profession, class year, location and name.

Career Center

The KU Alumni Career Center is designed to help alumni advance their career goals, with tools for job seeking Jayhawks and alumni employers.

KU Alumni Mentor Network

The KU Alumni Mentor Network offers alumni and students the opportunity to connect with fellow Jayhawks and share experiences, gain knowledge and build a professional network.

Kansas Alumni magazine

The Kansas Alumni magazine keeps alumni, parents and friends up-to-date on the latest University news. This award-winning publication prints six issues yearly and covers alumni from across the country, campus hot topics and breakthroughs in KU research.

KU Libraries Access

Thanks to a partnership with KU Libraries, KU Alumni Association members now have access to more than 50,000 magazines, journals and index records—invaluable library resources for alumni and friends who wish to continue their professional and personal development.

Shop for KU Merchandise

Proceeds from your purchase at the KU Alumni Association Store support the KU Alumni Association, providing programs for alumni, students and the entire KU community. We appreciate your loyalty!

Just for Fun

Find all kinds of fun Jayhawk things on our Just for Fun page—including printable activity and coloring sheets, crossword puzzles, Valentine cards, Halloween treat bag toppers, crafty tutorials, knit finger puppets, and an archive of our popular annual holiday cards.

KU Websites

Connect with other departments at the University of Kansas, including the KU Medical Center, KU Athletics, KU Endowment and more. Links to these and other KU websites can be found here.
