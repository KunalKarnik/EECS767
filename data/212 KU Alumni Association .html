Administration

 

Heath Peterson

President
heathpeterson@kualumni.org
Heath, d’04, g’09, a Hugoton native, guides the University of Kansas’ alumni relations strategy; oversees engagement initiatives with the Alumni Association’s 42,000 members; leads a 46-member staff; and manages the Alumni Association’s broad range of programs, benefits, services, and resources. He works closely with University and affiliate leadership on all campuses to strengthen and advance KU. He married a Jayhawk, Carrie Peterson DDS, and they have three children, Beau, Presley, and Claire.

 

Heather Hawkins

Executive Assistant to the President and Coordinator of Donor Relations
hrhawkins‌@kualumni.org
Heather, j’06, received her bachelor’s degree in strategic communication from KU in 2006 and received a master’s in educational leadership and management in 2012. Heather helps with projects for the Alumni Association’s Board of Directors and acts as a stewardship coordinator for donors. She is also in charge of the Fred Ellsworth Medallion celebration and helps the president whenever needed! When she’s not working, her husband Jeff, son Mavrick, daughter Sienna and their two boxers keep her on her toes!

Adams Alumni Center

 

Laurie Doud

Event Coordinator
ldoud@kualumni.org
Laurie, c’98, coordinates a wide variety of business and social events that take place in the center. A Wisconsin native, she earned a biology degree from KU as a non-traditional student. She occasionally brings in “Show ‘n’ Tell” (live or otherwise) from the natural world to share with her co-workers. If Laurie is not working or volunteering, you’ll find her outside somewhere.

 

Bryan Greve

Senior Vice President of Hospitality Services
bgreve@kualumni.org
Bryan loves to see other people having fun! A true hospitalitarian, Bryan has a degree from JCCC in Hospitality Management and another from Baker University. Bryan is also certified through the Club Managers Association of America. After working at private clubs in Kansas City for seven years; he wound up here in 1986 to help manage the new Adams Alumni Center. Throughout his tenure he has worked in several aspects of the Association, including our membership area, where he invented the Jayhawk Society. He enjoys playing and singing rock music with his son’s band, Church, motorcycles, John Wayne and his grandchildren.

 

Ben Shepley

Director of Adams Alumni Center
bshepley@kualumni.org
Ben directs the organization’s facilities department overseeing the functioning of building systems including mechanical, electrical, fire/life safety, elevators and the facilities renovation projects. He also manages the maintenance of the building and grounds. In his spare time he likes to play golf and spend quality time with his lovely wife, Lindsey, son Zach, and two daughters Brookelynn and Charlee.

 

Laura Stephens

Assistant Director of Hospitality Services
lstephens@kualumni.org
Laura joined the Association in 2013 after working for a local catering company in the sales and marketing department. As the Assistant Director for Hospitality Services, she coordinates staff and helps to plan and oversee events in the building. When not at work, she enjoys training and showing horses and keeping up with her busy family including her son Cooper.

Alumni and Student Programs

 

Brad Eland

Vice President of Alumni and Student Programs
beland@kualumni.org
Brad, b’09, g’11, oversees the Association’s alumni and student programs department. He works with a talented staff and hundreds of volunteers to reach students on campus and alumni across the country with creative programming. A Hoxie native and fourth-generation Jayhawk, Brad previously worked for Kansas Athletics in the marketing department and also spent time in Chicago working as a management consultant. He and his wife Laura, j’09, c’09, live in Lawrence with their son Theo. Brad enjoys traveling, reading, staying fit with a game of hoops, playing as much golf as possible and rooting on the Jayhawks.

 

Emily Ellison

Coordinator of Customer Service and Operations
emilyellison@kualumni.org
Emily, c’11, graduated from KU with a degree in Applied Behavioral Science with a focus in Community Health and Development. She showed her Rock Chalk pride as a student by appearing as Baby Jay. Following graduation, Emily moved back to Oregon, the land of Ducks and Beavers, and desperately missed her Jayhawks. After clicking her heels three times she came back and joined the Alumni Association in 2013. In her free time, Emily enjoys spending time with family and friends, cheering on her Jayhawks and going on spontaneous road trips.

 

Kelsey Hill

Assistant Director of Kansas City and Wichita Programs
kelseyhill@kualumni.org
Kelsey, c’12, a native of Hoxie, joined the Association in 2012. As a coordinator of alumni programs, she helps oversee national alumni networks and in the western half of Kansas. Kelsey is an avid Jayhawk fan and in her spare time attends as many events as possible. When she is not cheering on her Jayhawks, she enjoys reading, running and cooking.

 

Danielle Hoover

Assistant Director of Wichita Programs
dhoover@kualumni.org
Danielle Hoover, c’07, was born into a family of KU fans and raised in the Wichita area. Her passion for the Jayhawks started at an early age, and after earning a degree in communication studies, she returned to Wichita where she lives with her husband, Darrick; their son, Hudson, and daughter, Harlow; and their two cocker spaniels. One of her favorite memories is being present when KU won the 2008 National Championship. Danielle is excited to combine two things she loves—KU and Wichita—in her position.

 

Jacey Krehbiel

Assistant Director of Alumni Programs, Homecoming and Kansas Honors Program
jkrehbiel@kualumni.org
Jacey, d’12, received her degree in sport management from KU. As a coordinator of alumni programs, she primarily oversees the Association’s affinity networks, but also assists with memberships and event auctions. Originally from McPherson, Jacey was raised crimson and blue. As a student at KU, she never missed a men’s basketball or football game. When she’s not cheering on her beloved Jayhawks, she enjoys reading, cooking, shopping and spending time with friends and family.

 

Michelle Lang

Director of Academic Programs
michellem@kualumni.org
Michelle, b’01, heads up one of the Association’s oldest traditions, the Kansas Honors Program. Born and raised a KU fan, she enjoys traveling throughout the state and nationally to share her love of the University and connect with students and alumni. When she’s not spending time with her Jayhawk family, she is hanging out with her own family and friends.

 

Joy Maxwell

Director of Legacy Relations
jmaxwell@kualumni.org
Joy, c’03, j’03, directs legacy recruitment efforts for the KU Alumni Association. Joy works to identify and recruit Jayhawk hopefuls from KU families, promote the Jayhawk Generations Scholarship and engage alumni in steering top-notch students to Mount Oread. When she’s not cheering on her beloved ‘Hawks, she loves to travel and spend time with her family—husband Kevin, e’05, m’14, their sons, Luke and Wyatt, and daughter, Reece.

 

Tyler Rockers

Assistant Director of National and Kansas Alumni Networks
trockers@kualumni.org
Tyler, d’13, is a native of Greeley and grew up in a Jayhawk family. He joined the Association in 2013 as a coordinator of alumni programs after being involved as a student. Tyler oversees alumni networks in the eastern half of the state as well as across the country. When he is away from work, his hobbies include golf, aviation, reading and working on his beloved lawn. Tyler is a self-proclaimed history buff and an aspiring grillmaster, but the latter is still a work in progress.

 

Betsy Winetroub

Director of Kansas City Programs
bwinetroub@kualumni.org
Shortly after receiving her bachelor’s degree from KU in 2005, Betsy joined the Alumni Association’s membership team. She returned in 2012 for her dream job of working for KU in Kansas City as the director of Kansas City programs. Betsy leads the Kansas City network and connect local Jayhawks through events such as the KU Kickoff at Corinth Square and the Rock Chalk Ball. In her free time, Betsy loves to read, run, cuddle with her beautiful yellow lab, Huckleberry, and of course, cheer on her Jayhawks!

Communications

 

Jennifer Jackson Sanner

Senior Vice President for Strategic Communications and Advocacy, Editor of Kansas Alumni magazine
jjsanner@kualumni.org
Jennifer, j’81, joined the Association staff in 1984 as staff writer and stumbled into a career. She and her husband, Bob, b’80, have three children: Rachel and Jackson, both proud KU business graduates, and Claire, who will follow her siblings to the Hill in August as a freshman and a distance runner for the Jayhawks.

 

Heather Biele

Staff Writer
hbiele@kualumni.org
Heather graduated from Kansas State University in 1999 with a doctorate in veterinary medicine. After pursuing a number of traditional roles in the profession, she discovered what she truly loved was writing and editing. That, coupled with her love of Lawrence and the Jayhawks, made accepting a position on the Association’s communications team a no-brainer. In her free time, she enjoys hanging out downtown with friends, traveling and cheering on her two favorite teams—the Jayhawks and the St. Louis Cardinals.

 

Steven Hill

Associate Editor, Kansas Alumni magazine
shill@kualumni.org
Steve graduated from the University of North Carolina-Wilmington and holds a master’s degree from Columbia University. He has written for the Atlanta Journal-Constitution, the Chicago Tribune, Field & Stream and other publications. He enjoys cycling and hunting (not at the same time) and spending time with his wife, Mary, and their three young children.

 

Chris Lazzarino

Associate Editor, Kansas Alumni magazine
clazz@kualumni.org
Lazz, j’86, began his journalism career as a newspaper sportswriter in South Florida, and joined Kansas Alumni magazine after returning to his hometown to be close to family. He enjoys reading, photography, travel and long hikes with the world’s greatest dog, Parker.

 

Valerie Spicher

Graphic Designer
vspicher@kualumni.org
Valerie, j’94, designs a variety of publications for the Association, including brochures, booklets, posters, invitations, banners and anything you would want to put a Jayhawk on. Valerie dotes on her goofy Boston Terrier and enjoys reading and cooking.

 

Dan Storey

Staff Photographer and Videographer
dstorey@kualumni.org
Dan graduated from Colorado State University and joined the Association in 2013 after spending seven years with Kansas Athletics. He provides quality images, both moving and still, that showcase the Association’s many events. Outside the office, Dan enjoys travel, Jayhawk athletics events and outdoor activities. Dan and his wife Angie, b’04, g’07, have three daughters, Olivia, Eleanor and Ruby Cate.

 

Susan Younger

Creative Director
syounger@kualumni.org
Susan, f’91, is the Creative Director for the Association. She oversees publications including Kansas Alumni magazine. She also assists with branding and decor for special events. Susan has also served as president of UCDA, a national design organization. Both of her sons attended KU, and when she can pull herself away from work, she spends time in her glass studio or with her husband, Jerry, e’86, g’92.

Debbi Johanning

Digital Media Content Manager
djohanning@kualumni.org

She and her husband, former KU basketball player David Johanning, f’94, have a son, Cooper, and a rambunctious yellow labrador, Lucy. Debbi, a Wellington native, enjoys reading, live music and spending time with friends in her limited spare time. David Johnston

Vice President of Marketing and Digital Media
davidjohnston@kualumni.org

When the former KU cross country All-American isn’t out running, he usually can be found cheering on the Jayhawks with his wife, Sara, d’96, their two girls, Sydney and Sophia, and their little boy, Austin.

 

Kara Rodriguez

Digital Media and Marketing Coordinator
karaschwerdt@kualumni.org
Kara, j’10, graduated from KU with a journalism degree in Strategic Communications and joined the Association in 2014 to assist with broadcast emails and Kansas Alumni magazine advertising. Aside from cheering all things Jayhawk with her family, Kara enjoys painting, running, catering to her dog Mia and cat Tig, and being a newlywed with her husband, Cesar, c’12.

 

Michael Wick

Web Developer
mwick@kualumni.org
Mike, c’82, when he isn’t keeping the Web site running smoothly or assisting the less tech-savvy, devotes much of his time to his wife, Lisa, d’81, and his sons, Emmett and Travis.

 

Donor Relations

 

Angie Storey

Vice President of Donor Relations
angiestorey@kualumni.org
Angie, b’04, g’07, oversees the development efforts of the KU Alumni Association. A native of the south-central Kansas town of Sawyer, Angie has spent her career working on KU’s campus. She has previously held roles with Kansas Athletics and KU Endowment in addition to her current role with the Association. She and her husband, Dan, are busy raising three little future Jayhawks: Olivia, Eleanor and Ruby Cate.

 

Danny Lewis

Director of Donor Relations and Athletic Programs
dlewis@kualumni.org
Danny joined the Association after graduating from KU in 2004 with a degree in sports management. Originally from Surprise, Arizona, by way of St. Louis, Missouri, and Defiance, Ohio, he attended KU on a football scholarship. Danny ended his playing career by competing in the 2003 Tangerine Bowl. Danny and his wife, Laura, live in Eudora with their son, Carter, and daughter, Hadley. Any spare time is spent chasing kids around, planning their next adventure or in search of good food & drink.

Controller

Karen Whipple

Accountant

Marcia Wilson

Office Assistant

Membership and Business Development

 

Teri Harris

Vice President for Membership and Business Development
tharris@kualumni.org
A western Kansas native, Teri joined the Association in 2011 after spending 20 years in association management in the golf industry, where she focused on marketing, member/donor relations, corporate sales and fundraising. She received her bachelor’s degree in marketing from Emporia State University and has lived in Lawrence since 1987. Teri has one son, Kendall, who graduated from KU with a degree in sport management. She enjoys entertaining family and friends, watching KU sports and working out at the local gym.

 

Tegan Thornberry

Assistant Director of Membership and Business Development
tthornberry@kualumni.org
Tegan, d’05, g’09, received degrees in sport management and sport studies from KU. An Overland Park native, she lettered as a member of the women’s golf team and as a student manager for men’s basketball. Tegan oversees the Future Jayhawks and Flying Jayhawks programs and assists with membership efforts. She most recently worked for Kansas Athletics as director of team operations for swimming, diving, track and cross country, and previously served as travel coordinator. Tegan enjoys traveling, sporting events, the Kansas City Royals, all things KU, her nephew Carter Lewis and baking for “Tegan Tuesday.”
