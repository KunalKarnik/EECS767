Just for Fun

Home » Resources » Just for Fun

 

Flat Jayhawk

Take this printable flat Jayhawk on your adventures and snap a selfie with it! Click here to print your Jayhawk. Post your picture on our Facebook page, tag it on Twitter or Instagram with #kualumni, or email it to us at share@kualumni.org!

 

 

Activity Sheets

Entertain the kids (or yourself!) with our Jayhawk-themed activity sheets. Click here to see all seven downloadable sheets, including Tic-Tac-Toe, I Spy, two Jayhawk mazes and more! They’re perfect for long road trips.

 

 

Coloring Sheets

Our creative staff has designed eight different coloring sheets featuring our beloved mascot, including snowy scenes from the Hill and sporty ‘Hawks. Click here to download and print coloring sheets for your little Jayhawk—download one or print all eight!

 

 

Adult Coloring Sheet

Who said coloring is just for kids? Big and little Jayhawks can get in on the coloring action with our newly Jayhawk “adult coloring” sheet designed by Susan Younger, creative director. Download, print and color yours—and share the finished product with us at share@kualumni.org.

 

 

Knit finger puppets

Knit your own ‘Hawk and keep your fingers warm and stylish! These fun finger puppets make great stocking stuffers for your favorite Jayhawk. Click here for the pattern, created by Marilyn Harp.

 

 

Proud Member

Are you a Proud Member? Prove it to your social media followers!Download our graphic and share your Jayhawk pride on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram—use the hashtags #kualumni and #proudmember, and your picture will be included on our website.

 

 

KU Traditions Puzzle

Test your memory of KU traditions with our original Jayhawk crossword puzzle! Click here for a downloadable PDF of the puzzle. And don’t worry— if you get stumped, we’ll provide the answers.

 

 

Brick Library

For the crafty Jayhawks, we present a step-by-step tutorial for making your own brick “library,” as seen on the cover of the July, 2014, issue of Kansas Alumni magazine. Also a great gift idea for the book-loving Jayhawk in your life!

 

 

Jayhawk Valentine Cards

Our quick-fold Valentine cards are perfect for your Jayhawk sweetheart! Click here to choose from ten different designs to download and print. Perfect for school Valentine parties or the Future Jayhawk in your life.

 

 

Halloween Treat Toppers

Add some Jayhawk spirit to your Halloween treats with our printable bag toppers. Click here to download a printable PDF with the pattern; print as many as you need and then simply cut out, fold and staple onto your bag. Great for class parties or office treats!

 

 

Holiday Cards

Our creative staff enjoys creating fun holiday greetings for alumni. Click here to see our holiday cards from years past.

 

 

Send Holiday Greetings

We created fun, downloadable holiday cards that you can share with your friends on Facebook and Twitter! Click here to see all 12 cards and download your favorites.
