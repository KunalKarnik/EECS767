KU Libraries Access

Home » KU Libraries Access

For Members Only

Thanks to a partnership with KU Libraries, KU Alumni Association members now have access to more than 50,000 magazines, journals and index records—invaluable library resources for alumni and friends who wish to continue their professional and personal development.

Association members can access three databases within KU Libraries. These resources include the latest scholarly information in many areas of academic study, full-text business magazines and journals, and comprehensive, yet concise summaries of the best business books available.

Resources are freely available to registered members at www.kualumni.org/libraries. Not a member? Join now at www.kualumni.org/join. Library access will be available 1-2 business days after you join.

Important Information Regarding Access

To access these resources, users must:

  • Be a member in the KU Alumni Association
  • Obtain a username and password by registering online at www.kualumni.org.

 

To access all three resources, log in or register below. Not a member? Join today!

Access to journals, magazines, and other sources subject to change without notice.

 

KU Libraries Resources Available to KU Alumni Association Members

Academic Search Alumni Edition

“With a broad academic scope, this comprehensive database offers alumni an invaluable tool for their post-college pursuits. It provides full text as well as indexing and abstracting for thousands of peer-reviewed journals and general periodicals.”

This resource includes:

  • More than 13,780 indexed and abstracted journals
  • Full text for more than 2,640 journals
  • More than 2,280 peer-reviewed full-text journals

This resource offers the latest scholarly, full-text information in many areas of academic study to meet virtually any researcher’s needs. Designed for the academic-based research needs of the post-college professional, this database provides full text for thousands of peer-reviewed journals—as well as thousands of peer-reviewed indexed and abstracted journals.  Additionally, many popular magazines are included in this database for general interest pursuits.

Business Source Alumni Edition

This database provides more than 1,390 full-text business magazines and journals, of which over 650 are peer-reviewed. Business Source Alumni Edition includes publications in nearly every area of business including marketing, management, MIS, POM, accounting, finance, econometrics, economics and more. Business Source Alumni Edition includes full-text sources ranging from general periodicals to trade publications and top management journals. Additionally there are over 29,000 full-text records, which include country economic reports, industry reports, market research reports, company profiles and more.

Business Book Summaries

This database keeps researchers abreast of the latest in business intelligence and trends by providing comprehensive, yet concise summaries for the best business books available. This remarkable resource produces summaries of 1,800 of the top business books from a wide range of important subject areas.

All books covered by Business Book Summaries (BBS) are summarized by professionals. The most salient points are extracted and conceptualized into brief, but comprehensive, summaries. By presenting key concepts and relevant business thoughts, people with no time to read the entire book can retain valuable information and apply it to their jobs.

Each summary includes:

  • Brief abstract of each book
  • Comprehensive summary of each book
  • Summaries for thousands of the top business books from the last 20 years, with new books added daily
  • Audio versions of the summaries, when available and mobile access from most devices
