National Networks

Home » Networks and Clubs » National Networks

The KU Alumni Association offers networks across the country and around the world. These groups get together to watch games, enjoy KU camaraderie, recruit the best incoming students to KU and work to strengthen the University.

Click on the map below to find a network near you. For more information or to start a new network in your area, contact Danny Lewis, director of alumni programs, at dlewis@kualumni.org.
