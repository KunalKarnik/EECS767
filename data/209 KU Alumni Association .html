Watch Sites by State

Watch sites are designated spots to watch the ‘Hawks with fellow KU alumni and fans. A list of official KU alumni watch sites—organized alphabetically by state—is below, with each site linked to a map.

Official watch parties organized by the local network will be listed on our online calendar. If there isn’t an official party scheduled, we cannot guarantee that the watch sites below will show every KU game, especially if there are other college or professional sporting events happening at the same time.

Please reach out to local volunteers listed below if you have questions about a watch site, or visit our Alumni Networks page to find links to network websites and Facebook groups.
