    Why I Give

    Last year, donors supported KU with more than $258.8 million in donations. State financing now covers less than 20 percent of KU’s budget, making private support more vital than ever. Large or small, every gift contributes to building a greater KU.

    Far Above: The Campaign for Kansas is the university's comprehensive fundraising campaign. The campaign’s bold vision will enable students to excel, and bring prosperity and well-being to communities in Kansas and beyond.
