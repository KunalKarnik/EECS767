University of Kansas School of Nursing

Shaping the future of nursing

Trusted by patients and critical to the health care team, nurses make a meaningful difference in the lives of patients and their families every day.  

At the University of Kansas School of Nursing, with nationally ranked programs and a rich history of quality education, students gain the knowledge and skills they need to build dynamic careers as clinicians, educators, researchers and leaders. 

The KU School of Nursing, on the campus of the premier University of Kansas Medical Center, offers innovative interprofessional programs in every field of study. Students learn in an academic environment that ensures access to top-notch faculty, state-of-the-art technology and clinical experiences in diverse practice settings.

Designated a Center of Excellence in Nursing Education by the National League for Nursing, the KU School of Nursing remains the only school in the region to be recognized by the NLN for its commitment to innovation and excellence in the education of nurses.
