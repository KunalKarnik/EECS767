Attention Principle Investigators/Lab Managers

Beginning in January 2016, the New Animal User Training Form and the Tri-Annual Training Form will become completely electronic. No paper copies of these forms will be accepted. Any questions can be directed to Larissa Dobbeleare or John Hinkle.

All electronic forms must be opened in Internet Explorer and completed by the individual that will be attending the training, not another individual in the lab. Links will be provided for these forms in the same locations as the old paper versions of the forms.


Mission

 To foster a philosophy of compassion and respect for animals used in research, provide participants with the necessary knowledge base and technical skills to carry out research procedures.


Vision

The KUMC Laboratory Animal Resources Department is a quality animal care and use program known for its animal welfare oriented environment, talent management, research environment flexibility and operational efficiency.

Please use the LAR Service Feedback Form to share your compliments, complaints and suggestions. We welcome your feedback as it will help us improve the quality of our information, and services.
