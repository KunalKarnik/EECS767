Weddings

Home » About » Adams Alumni Center » Weddings

An alluring atmosphere and convenient campus location make the Adams Alumni Center a one-of-a-kind setting for couples to enjoy a memorable wedding ceremony, reception or rehearsal dinner. Several wedding packages are available.

Wedding Reception Package
First Floor $800
Second Floor $1,800

Our wedding reception package includes everything you need, free of any mumbo jumbo.

  • Rental of the entire first or second floor
  • Professional staff on site to attend to your needs throughout the entire event
  • Use of tables and chairs, including setup and removal
  • Linens for guest tables, cocktail tables, guestbook table and DJ table
  • Table skirting
  • Use of TVs, wireless microphone, projector and hookups
  • Dance floor (12×12 or 12×15)
  • Bridal party prep rooms (Music Room or Library)
  • House music
  • Complimentary room for rehearsal dinner with reception booking
  • Free limited accessible parking with reserved space for bride and groom*
  • Additional parking located directly across the street in the Union parking garage

 

Wedding Ceremony Package: $500

Just looking for a place to have your ceremony? Our wedding ceremony package includes rental of the first-floor ceremony area and bridal prep rooms, along with the following:

  • Professional staff on site to attend to your needs during rehearsal and ceremony
  • Setup and removal of chairs
  • Unity table with white linen cloth
  • Use of outdoor terrace
  • Bridal party prep rooms
  • House music
  • Wireless microphone
  • Free limited accessible parking with reserved space for bride and groom*
  • Additional parking located directly across the street in the Union parking garage

Combined Wedding Ceremony & Reception Package: $2,200

This combined package includes all items from the Wedding Ceremony and Wedding Reception packages. Contact our staff at 800.584.2957, 785.864.4760 or kualumni@kualumni.org for more information.

 

*Parking is available for guests on a first-come, first-serve basis. Additional parking is available across the street in the Kansas Union parking garage.
