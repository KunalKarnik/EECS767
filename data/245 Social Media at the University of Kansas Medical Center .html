Social Media at the University of Kansas Medical Center

Social media outlets are an excellent way to provide current updates and two-way communication with constituents of the University of Kansas Medical Center. Schools, departments and KU Medical Center organizations must follow rules that apply to traditional media outlets as well as unique rules associated with social media.

Our social media policy applies to the schools, departments and registered student organizations that identify themselves as being a part of the University of Kansas Medical Center and use its branding (title, letterhead, signature file, logos, web domain, etc.) in public outlets.
