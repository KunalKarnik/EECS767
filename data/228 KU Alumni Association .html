Adams Alumni Center

Home » About » Adams Alumni Center


 

Welcome Home, ‘Hawks!

The Adams Alumni Center, constructed in 1983, is located at 1266 Oread Avenue on the northeast corner of the University of Kansas campus, across from the Kansas Union parking garage. The building is open to the public and our office hours are Monday-Friday 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Rental Facilities

The convenient campus location makes the Adams Alumni Center an ideal spot for meetings, wedding ceremonies, receptions, dinners and more. Rooms are also available for University-related functions and are located within walking distance of Danforth Chapel.

Whether you’re hosting a family celebration or a corporate meeting, we have the space and staff to make any function a success. Depending on the configuration and event, the center can hold as many as 250 people.

KU Alumni Association

The third floor of the Adams Alumni center is the home of the KU Alumni Association’s national office.
