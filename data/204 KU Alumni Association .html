Networks and Clubs

Home » Networks and Clubs

You may have left the Hill, but there are plenty of ways to connect with Jayhawks! The KU Alumni Association offers networks and clubs throughout the state, across the country and around the world. These groups get together to watch games, enjoy KU camaraderie, recruit the best incoming students to KU and work to strengthen the University.

Networks and clubs also exist for alumni to share their affinity for a group, such as the Black Alumni Network, Hispanic Alumni Network, Veterans Alumni Network and Band Alumni Network.

All of our networks and clubs have Facebook groups where alumni, fans and friends can connect with each other. Networks also have an official website with network leader contact information, events and more.
