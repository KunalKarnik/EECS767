  • KU Songbird by Susan Younger

    155 views 1 month ago
    "KU Songbird' is a tribute to KU’s alma mater, ‘Crimson and the Blue,’ which was written in 1891 by Professor George Barlow Penny. All three stanzas of the song are shown. The swirling design with shiny glass mosaic evokes movement and is reminiscent of a bandana, which pays homage to our Midwest heritage.
    Susan Younger, f‘91, is the creative director for the KU Alumni Association and designs publications, including the Kansas Alumni magazine. She also creates set and table designs for the Association’s major annual events, including the Rock Chalk Ball in Kansas City and the Jayhawk Roundup in Wichita. Show less
