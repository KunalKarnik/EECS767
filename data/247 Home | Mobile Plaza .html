Don't Miss the Bus!

The Where’s My Bus? app allows KU students, faculty and staff to quickly and easily track the location of buses and view scheduled and estimated pick-up times (based on current location of the bus on its route). The app is available for download on iPhone and Android phones.

Blackboard Mobile Learn

Blackboard Mobile™ Learn extends the existing KU Blackboard learning system by making much of the core content available across your Blackboard courses, content and organizations, in an engaging and intuitive way.

myKU Mobile Site

The myKU mobile portal is the one-stop-shop for all your KU online needs. Now, you don’t have to remember or bookmark link after link to KU websites and resources. In myKU, you can access Enroll & Pay, Parking, technology resources, KU Card Center and more. The myKU portal is even personalized with access to your KU email and calendar.

KU Athletics

This is the official mobile app of the Kansas Jayhawks. Make your iPhone or iPod Touch a unique part of your game-day experience for Jayhawk events. Want to catch breaking news of the team? See updated statistics? Access video on-demand and live video streams? Follow pre-game previews of the matchups and read game recaps? Re-live each event with photo galleries.

Connect at KU with Skype for Business

Skype for Business (Microsoft) is powerful way to instantly communicate with KU students and colleagues, as well as others around the world. With the click of a button you can open a text chat, make a voice or video call, or even start an online meeting. Skype for Business makes online communication more collaborative and engaging. And, you can use Skype for Business from nearly any computer or mobile device, on campus or anywhere in the world you have Internet access.
