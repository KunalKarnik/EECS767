Kansas Networks

Home » Networks and Clubs » Kansas Networks

You may have left the Hill, but there are plenty of ways to connect with Jayhawks! The KU Alumni Association offers 22 networks throughout the state of Kansas. Each of the state’s 105 counties is assigned to a network area.

Click on a county below to find your network. For more information about Kansas networks, contact one of the staff members listed on the right side of the page. Or, you can contact Brad Eland, vice president of alumni programs, at beland@kualumni.org.
